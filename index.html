ID Photo 4×6 Layout

35×45 mm • 3×2 grid • Outer ticks Works offline • No upload
Tip: use an edited, properly cropped headshot (35×45 mm aspect ≈ 0.78). The app will place 6 copies.
Print settings: Paper 4×6 (10×15 cm), Scale 100%, Borderless OFF.

What it does

  • Creates a true 4×6 inch sheet at chosen DPI (600/300)
  • Places six copies of your photo (35×45 mm each)
  • Draws outer corner ticks 6 top + 6 bottom + 4 left + 4 right
  • All local — no upload
Made for Canon SELPHY CP1500. Keep Borderless OFF to preserve exact sizes & ticks.
